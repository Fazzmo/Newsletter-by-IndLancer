📸 Instagram sources work with a free Meta Access Token. This lets you follow any public Business or Creator account — including regional news creators. You only need to set this up once.
  1. Go to developers.facebook.com/apps and create a free app
  2. Add the "Instagram" product to your app
  3. Connect your own Instagram Creator/Business account
  4. Generate an access token from the Graph API Explorer
  5. Paste it below — it's stored only in your browser
Now add any Instagram Business or Creator account username you want in your digest. Works with regional news creators, journalists, brands — anyone with a professional account.
@bbcnews
@ndtv
@timesofindia

Only works with Business or Creator accounts — not personal accounts. Most news creators qualify.

✉️ How email sending works — EmailJS
We use EmailJS — a free service that sends emails directly from your browser with no server. Free plan gives you 200 emails/month (plenty for a daily digest). One-time setup takes about 5 minutes:
  1. Sign up free at emailjs.com
  2. Create an Email Service — connect your Gmail or Outlook
  3. Create an Email Template — use variables {{subject}} and {{html_content}}
  4. Copy your Service ID, Template ID, and Public Key below
